## Method and calibration

`lean_source_scanner.py` masks line comments, nested block comments, and
escaped strings without changing offsets, then labels a raw match executable
only when its span survives in masked code.  It rejects symlink inputs,
sorts inputs and hits deterministically, records one-based locations, and
fails on lexical diagnostics.

The fresh calibration has two independent forms:

- [recert_source_scanner_calibration.log](logs/recert_source_scanner_calibration.log)
  records seven passing unit tests, including nested comments/strings,
  non-code negatives, lexical-error detection, the known current Exercise
  positive at `Exercise/Chapter1/Sec01.lean:37`, and confirmation that the
  former Gaussian-Chevet positive is now absent.
- [recert_v3_calibration.json](logs/recert_v3_calibration.json), generated by
  the [calibration command](logs/recert_v3_calibration_command.log), scans the
  fresh recertification positive (born after the pass start; see the
  [scratch birth audit](logs/recert_v5_scratch_birth_audit.log)) plus the
  known Exercise leaf.  It finds the planted
  `admit`, `sorryAx`, `proof_wanted`, `#exit`, and `stop`, both executable
  `sorry` sites, and every marker-class positive.

Thus zero counts below are backed by detectors that fired on known or
planted positives in this session.

## Limitations

The source scanner is syntactic, although calibrated for Lean comments,
strings, offsets, and malformed lexical input.  V4's `Lean.collectAxioms`
environment audit supplies the complementary transitive kernel check; the
current-tree two-way join is reported above.

The 228 Exercise gaps are accepted only as `KNOWN-LEDGERED` debt, not as
proved mathematics.  V3 does not assess the faithfulness, non-vacuity, or
semantic strength of sorry-free statements.  Scratch, Verification code, the
root import module, and pinned dependency packages are outside the stated
FILE-WALK source census.
